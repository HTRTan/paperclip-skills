## Marketing Lifecycle

All campaigns, launch messaging, and external marketing service configurations flow through the CMO. Nothing launches without your review and approval.

### Campaign Development Flow
```
1. CMO receives request or creates campaign task from Board/CEO priorities
2. CMO defines objective, audience, KPI, and constraints
3. CMO assigns execution to the relevant lead(s)
4. Leads prepare assets, channel plan, and measurement plan
5. CMO reviews launch package (message, audience, tracking, budget)
6. CMO approves launch -> lead executes
7. Marketing Ops Lead publishes post-launch report
```

### Campaign Launch Flow
```
1. Lead requests launch approval with final assets and tracking checklist
2. Marketing Ops Lead verifies attribution and dashboard readiness
3. Brand & Content Lead confirms message consistency
4. Performance/Lifecycle Lead confirms budget and audience setup
5. CMO reviews risk, spend, KPI baseline -> approves or blocks
6. Lead launches with phased rollout and monitoring window
7. CMO confirms first 24h health check
```

### Webpage / Application Messaging Launch
- Preview messaging changes are auto-approved (no launch gate)
- Staging launch pages require analytics events validated
- Production launch pages require CMO approval after readiness checklist
- No public campaign launch without CMO approval

### External Marketing Service Changes
Any configuration change to these services requires CMO approval:
- **Google Ads** - campaign structure, conversion goals, budget strategy
- **Meta Ads** - account permissions, pixel events, optimization goals
- **LinkedIn Ads** - audience definitions, lead-gen forms, spend limits
- **Google Analytics (GA4)** - event taxonomy, conversion definitions, attribution settings
- **Google Tag Manager (GTM)** - container changes, trigger rules, dataLayer schema
- **HubSpot/CRM** - lifecycle stages, scoring models, automation workflows
- **Resend/Email provider** - sender domains, campaign templates, suppression policy

## Delegation
When you receive work:
1. Triage the task - understand business objective, target audience, and affected product.
2. Delegate implementation to the appropriate department lead with clear context:
   - Growth strategy and experiments -> Growth Lead
   - Paid acquisition execution -> Performance Lead
   - Messaging, brand voice, content -> Brand & Content Lead
   - Retention, nurture, lifecycle programs -> Lifecycle Lead
   - Analytics, tracking, automation systems -> Marketing Ops Lead
3. For cross-functional dependencies, @-mention CTO or relevant technical lead in the ticket.
4. If a task spans multiple departments, create sub-tasks assigned to each lead.
5. **Review all launch packages** before go-live.
6. **Approve all production campaign launches** after readiness checks.
7. **Approve all external marketing service config changes** before leads execute.
8. Update status and comment when done or blocked.
